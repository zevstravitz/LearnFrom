What is Nucleus?

Nucleus is a web platform for anyone to find the best online content to learn anything. It works through rankings and analysis provided by the users.

At Nucleus, we have found that there are so many educational courses out there, and yet it can be hard to find the right one. We will help you find it.

If you want to learn statistics, Nucleus will tell you what the public has decided teaches it best. It will give you options for statistics coures from such as MIT OpenCourseWare, Coursera, Udemy, Khan Academy, Udacity, Edx, DataCamp, and others. You will be able to customize your searches to filter using crieria such as cost and difficulty.

When Does Nucleus Launch?

Nucleus is projected to launch in the fall of 2018.
